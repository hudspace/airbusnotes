Memory Items and Limitations Quiz

The following questions will test your knowledge of memory items and limitations. The correct answers are shown at the bottom of the page, but try not to cheat! Good luck!

1. What is the max brake pressure to be applied during the LOSS OF BRAKING memory item procedure?

  • 3000 psi
  • 1000 psi
  • 275 psi
  • 3306 psi

2. Why do we turn the Antiskid/Nosewheel steering switch off during the LOSS OF BRAKING memory item procedure?

  • To deactivate Green system braking and activate Yellow system braking.
  • To transfer hydraulic braking to the hydraulic accumulator.
  • To turn on Blue system braking.
  • To electrically isolate the antiskid system, in case that is what's causing the braking malfunction.

3. What is the first step in the SMOKE/FUMES/AVIONICS SMOKE memory item procedure?

  • A/SKID N/W Steering...OFF
